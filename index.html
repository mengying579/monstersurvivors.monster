About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive survival game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible while facing waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and test your skills in this action-packed adventure.

With simple controls and challenging gameplay, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment as you try to beat your high score and discover new strategies to survive longer.

How To Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby monsters
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose powerful upgrades as you level
  • Survive as long as possible against endless waves

Game Features

  • Roguelike survival gameplay
  • Multiple character abilities and upgrades
  • Various enemy types with different behaviors
  • Increasing difficulty as time progresses
  • Exciting power-ups and special weapons

Tips & Strategies

Mastering Monster Survivors requires both strategy and quick reflexes. Here are some tips to help you survive longer:

  • Keep moving constantly to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Focus on area damage abilities when facing large groups
  • Collect all experience orbs to level up faster
  • Use terrain and obstacles to funnel enemies and create chokepoints
